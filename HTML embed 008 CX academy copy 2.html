Curriculum

Part 1: Understanding The Impact of Customer Experience Why we call Customer Experience a growth engine. Brief history of Customer Success. How Customer Experience has evolved in Visma. How a Visma company has implemented a Customer Success based business model. The e-learning module will take around 40 minutes to complete.

Part 2: Customer Journey Understanding the customer journey allows you to deliver the highest CX quality by focusing on continually improving all of a customer’s interactions and making CX a competitive differentiator. This module will teach you how to map a customer journey. The e-learning module will take around 35 minutes to complete.

Part 3: Customer Experience metrics You can only improve what you measure. How can you find out what your customers think about you, your services and products? How to use the data to drive improvements. Benchmarks within Visma and the industry. The e-learning module will take around 25 minutes to complete.

Part 4: Customer Segmentation & Success Plans What is customer segmentation and how can it help you scale and plan your tasks and resources for maximum growth. How customer Success Plans help your customers achieve their business outcomes, and help you increase recurring revenue. The e-learning module will take around 20 minutes to complete.

Part 5: Organisation Structure for Customer Success & Channel strategy How to mobilize organisation for a CX based business model. Breaking down internal silos. Most relevant roles and their responsibilities to create a customer-centric organization. The e-learning module will take around 40 minutes to complete.

Part 6: Voice of the Customer How Voice of the Customer framework sets up the organisation for sustainable improvements of customer experience. How VoC allows organisation to consistently respond to market requirements and fuel growth. The e-learning module will take around 20 minutes to complete
