A new way to define Customer Success Day Zero

Day Zero is the moment when a customer has completed the necessary tasks so they can start to realize the full value of your product for the job they are hiring it to do.

More information here: [https://www.intercom.com/blog/day-zero-a-new-way-to-define-customer-success/]

Customers who have reached Day zero are willing to pay 30% more, Want more services from us, Have a higher level of acceptance and loyalty, Have a better overall customer experience, ...which means business opportunities!

Customers are more likely to churn if they don’t reach Day Zero, or don’t reach it fast enough.

The customer is able to begin realizing value
